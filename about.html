A GUIDE FOR POTENTIAL USER OF THE UNSENT PROJECT





THIS IS A PLACE WHERE YOU CAN SHARE YOUR UNSAID THOUGHTS


Our team designed the Unsent Project website to provide Quezon City University students with a safe and friendly space.
It's a safe place to express thoughts, emotions, and sentiments that you might not feel comfortable chatting about with
others. Whether it's a secret you've been holding onto, a confession you're not quite ready to make public, or simply
words you wish you could say to someone, you can share it here anonymously. We've ensured that your privacy is our top
priority so you can feel safe and supported.
