A GUIDE FOR POTENTIAL USER OF THE UNSENT PROJECT



THIS IS A PLACE WHERE YOU CAN SHARE YOUR UNSAID THOUGHTS


Our group created the Unsent Project website to provide a safe space for students of Quezon City University who wants to

express their thoughts, feelings, and deepest emotions without the fear of judgement.

This website captures the unfiltered emotions of people from diverse backgrounds,

creating a meaningful collective narrative of human experiences.

By allowing anonymous posts, the website offers an assurance to users that they can share their stories and experiences

without the need of revealing their identities.

Fostering a sense of support system among users who may be going through similar experiences,

and helping them to feel less alone and understood.
